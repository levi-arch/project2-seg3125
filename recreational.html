About Canadian Soccer Academy

The Canadian Soccer Academy is committed to providing a nurturing and inclusive environment for young players to develop their soccer skills. Our recreational soccer program focuses on building a love for the sport, teamwork, and fostering a positive learning experience.

Our Recreational Soccer Program

Recreational Soccer for Children

Our recreational soccer program is designed for children aged 6 to 12 years who want to learn and enjoy soccer in a non-competitive setting. Our certified coaches focus on teaching basic skills, teamwork, and fair play while ensuring a fun and engaging experience for all participants.
